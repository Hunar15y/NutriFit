Leg Workout

Squats

3 sets of 8-12 reps

Leg Press

3 sets of 10-15 reps

Keep your back flat against the seat.

Deadlifts

3 sets of 8-10 reps

Engage your glutes and hamstrings.

Lunges

3 sets of 10-12 reps (each leg)

Keep your front knee aligned with your ankle.

Leg Curls

3 sets of 10-15 reps

Control the weight both up and down.
