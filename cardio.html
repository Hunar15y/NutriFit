Cardio Workout

Workout Routine

1. Running

Duration: 20-30 minutes

Instructions: Maintain a steady pace; try interval training for increased intensity.

2. Jump Rope

Sets: 5 | Duration: 1 minute

Instructions: Jump continuously at a moderate pace, rest for 30 seconds between sets.

3. Cycling

Duration: 30-60 minutes

Instructions: Use a stationary bike or cycle outdoors at a moderate pace.

4. HIIT Workouts

Duration: 20-30 minutes

Instructions: Alternate between high-intensity exercises and rest periods.

5. Burpees

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-15

Instructions: From standing, drop into a squat, kick your feet back into a plank, and return to standing.

6. Jumping Jacks

Sets: 3 | Duration: 1 minute

Instructions: Jump while spreading your arms and legs, then return to the starting position.

7. High Knees

Sets: 3 | Duration: 30 seconds

Instructions: Run in place, bringing your knees up towards your chest as high as possible.
