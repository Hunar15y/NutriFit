Full Body Workout

Workout Routine

1. Push-Ups

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15-20

Instructions: Perform standard push-ups. Keep your back straight and engage your core.

2. Squats

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12-15

Instructions: Lower into a squat, ensuring your knees don't go past your toes.

3. Burpees

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-15

Instructions: From standing, drop into a squat, kick your feet back into a plank, and return to standing.

4. Lunges

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12 per leg

Instructions: Step forward with one leg, lower your hips, and switch legs.

5. Plank

Sets: 3 | Hold for 30-60 seconds

Instructions: Hold a plank position, keeping your body in a straight line.
