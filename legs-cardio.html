2. Lunges

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12 per leg

Instructions: Step forward with one leg, lower your hips, and switch legs.

3. Bulgarian Split Squat

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10 per leg

Instructions: Place one foot behind you on a bench or platform, then squat down on the front leg.

4. Calf Raises

Sets: 4 | Reps: 15-20

Instructions: Stand on your toes and lift your heels off the ground.

Cardio Workout

1. Running

Duration: 20-30 minutes

Instructions: Maintain a steady pace; try interval training for increased intensity.

2. Jump Rope

Sets: 5 | Duration: 1 minute

Instructions: Jump continuously at a moderate pace, rest for 30 seconds between sets.

3. Cycling

Duration: 30-60 minutes

Instructions: Use a stationary bike or cycle outdoors at a moderate pace.

4. Burpees

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-15

Instructions: From standing, drop into a squat, kick your feet back into a plank, and return to standing.
