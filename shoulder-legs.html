Shoulders and Legs Workout

Workout Routine

1. Shoulder Press

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12-15

Instructions: Press dumbbells overhead from shoulder height and return to starting position.

2. Squats

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15-20

Instructions: Lower your body until your thighs are parallel to the ground, then stand back up.

3. Lateral Raises

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12-15

Instructions: Lift dumbbells to your sides until your arms are parallel to the ground, then lower them.

4. Lunges

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12-15 each leg

Instructions: Step forward and lower your body until both knees are bent at 90-degree angles.

5. Front Raises

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12-15

Instructions: Raise dumbbells in front of you to shoulder height, then lower them back down.

6. Deadlifts

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12

Instructions: Bend at the hips to lower the barbell, then stand up straight.

7. Calf Raises

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15-20

Instructions: Stand on your toes, hold, and lower your heels to the ground.
