Core Workout

Workout Routine

1. Plank

Sets: 3 | Duration: 30-60 seconds

Instructions: Maintain a straight line from head to heels while resting on your forearms and toes.

2. Russian Twists

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15-20

Instructions: Sit on the ground, lean back slightly, and twist your torso to touch the ground beside you with your hands.

3. Bicycle Crunches

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15-20

Instructions: Lie on your back and bring opposite elbow to knee while pedaling your legs.

4. Mountain Climbers

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15-20

Instructions: Start in a plank position and drive your knees towards your chest alternately.

5. Leg Raises

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-15

Instructions: Lie on your back and lift your legs towards the ceiling, then lower them without touching the ground.

6. Flutter Kicks

Sets: 3 | Duration: 30 seconds

Instructions: Lie on your back and alternate kicking your legs up and down while keeping them straight.

7. Side Plank

Sets: 3 | Duration: 20-30 seconds each side

Instructions: Lie on your side, resting on one forearm, and raise your body so that it forms a straight line.
