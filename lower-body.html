Lower Body Workout

Workout Routine

1. Squats

Sets: 4 | Reps: 10-15

Instructions: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower your body as if sitting back into a chair.

2. Lunges

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12 each leg

Instructions: Step forward with one leg, lower your hips until both knees are bent at about a 90-degree angle.

3. Deadlifts

Sets: 4 | Reps: 8-10

Instructions: Stand with feet hip-width apart, grip the barbell and lift it by extending your hips and knees.

4. Glute Bridges

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12-15

Instructions: Lie on your back, feet flat on the floor, lift your hips by squeezing your glutes.

5. Bulgarian Split Squats

Sets: 3 | Reps: 8-10 each leg

Instructions: Rest one foot on a bench behind you, lower your body with the other leg into a squat.

6. Step-Ups

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12 each leg

Instructions: Step up onto a bench or box with one leg, driving through the heel, then step down and repeat with the other leg.
