Yoga Workout

Workout Routine

1. Downward Dog

Hold: 30-60 seconds

Instructions: Start in a plank position, then lift your hips towards the ceiling to form an inverted V.

2. Warrior II

Hold: 30-60 seconds each side

Instructions: Stand with legs wide, turn one foot out, bend the knee of that leg, arms extended at shoulder height.

3. Tree Pose

Hold: 30-60 seconds each side

Instructions: Stand on one leg, place the other foot on the inner thigh or calf, bring hands together at chest level.

4. Bridge Pose

Hold: 30-60 seconds

Instructions: Lie on your back, bend your knees and lift your hips while pressing feet and arms into the floor.

5. Child's Pose

Hold: 30-60 seconds

Instructions: Sit back on your heels, stretch your arms forward on the ground, and relax your forehead on the floor.
