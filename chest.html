Chest Workout

Bench Press

3 sets of 8-12 reps

Focus on controlled movements and full range of motion.

Incline Dumbbell Press

3 sets of 10-15 reps

Keep your elbows at a 45-degree angle to protect your shoulders.

Chest Fly

3 sets of 10-12 reps

Ensure you maintain a slight bend in your elbows throughout.

Pec Deck Machine

3 sets of 10-15 reps

Adjust the seat height for proper alignment.

Push-Ups

3 sets of as many reps as possible

Maintain a straight line from head to heels.
