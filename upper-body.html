Upper Body Workout

Workout Routine

1. Push-Ups

Sets: 4 | Reps: 8-12

Instructions: Maintain a straight line from head to heels, lower your body until your chest almost touches the ground.

2. Bent Over Rows

Sets: 4 | Reps: 10-12

Instructions: Bend at the hips and knees, hold the barbell/dumbbells and pull them to your waist.

3. Dumbbell Shoulder Press

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12

Instructions: Stand or sit with dumbbells at shoulder height, press them overhead until arms are fully extended.

4. Tricep Dips

Sets: 3 | Reps: 8-12

Instructions: Use a bench or sturdy chair to lower your body by bending your elbows, then push back up.

5. Bicep Curls

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-15

Instructions: Stand with a dumbbell in each hand, curl the weights up towards your shoulders.

6. Lateral Raises

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12

Instructions: Stand with a dumbbell in each hand, lift them out to the sides until they're parallel to the ground.

7. Plank to Push-Up

Sets: 3 | Reps: 8-10

Instructions: Start in plank position, lower down into a push-up, and return to plank.
