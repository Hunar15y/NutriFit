Shoulder Workout

Overhead Press

3 sets of 8-12 reps

Keep your core tight and back straight.

Lateral Raises

3 sets of 10-15 reps

Lift to shoulder height with a slight bend in your elbows.

Front Raises

3 sets of 10-15 reps

Control the weight as you lift and lower.

Face Pulls

3 sets of 12-15 reps

Focus on squeezing your shoulder blades together.

Upright Rows

3 sets of 10-12 reps

Use a close grip and pull to your chin.
