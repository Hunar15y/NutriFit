Back and Biceps Workout

Workout Routine

1. Pull-Ups

Sets: 3 | Reps: 5-10

Instructions: Hang from a bar and pull your chin above it using your back muscles.

2. Bent Over Rows

Sets: 3 | Reps: 8-10

Instructions: Bend at the hips and pull weights towards your torso while keeping your back straight.

3. Deadlifts

Sets: 3 | Reps: 8-10

Instructions: Stand with feet hip-width apart, grip the barbell, and lift it while keeping your back straight.

4. Bicep Curls

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12

Instructions: Stand with dumbbells at your sides and curl them up towards your shoulders.

5. Hammer Curls

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12

Instructions: Hold dumbbells with your palms facing inwards and curl them towards your shoulders.

6. Lat Pulldowns

Sets: 3 | Reps: 8-10

Instructions: Sit at a lat pulldown machine and pull the bar down towards your chest.

7. Face Pulls

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-15

Instructions: Use a cable machine to pull the rope towards your face while keeping your elbows high.
