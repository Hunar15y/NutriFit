Active Recovery

Active Recovery Routine

1. Light Jogging

Duration: 10-15 minutes

Instructions: Keep a steady, relaxed pace to promote circulation and muscle recovery.

2. Dynamic Stretching

Duration: 5-10 minutes

Instructions: Perform leg swings, arm circles, and hip rotations to enhance flexibility.

3. Yoga

Duration: 20-30 minutes

Instructions: Focus on deep breathing and gentle stretches to relax muscles.

4. Foam Rolling

Duration: 5-10 minutes

Instructions: Gently roll over tight muscles to reduce soreness and improve mobility.

5. Walking

Duration: 20-30 minutes

Instructions: Maintain a relaxed pace to encourage blood flow and aid recovery.
