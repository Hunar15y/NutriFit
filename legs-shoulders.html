Legs and Shoulders Workout

Workout Routine

1. Squats

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12

Instructions: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower your body by bending your knees, and keep your back straight.

2. Deadlifts

Sets: 3 | Reps: 8-10

Instructions: Stand with feet hip-width apart, grip the barbell, and lift it while keeping your back straight.

3. Lunges

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12 each leg

Instructions: Step forward with one leg and lower your hips until both knees are bent at about a 90-degree angle.

4. Shoulder Press

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12

Instructions: Stand or sit with a dumbbell in each hand and press them overhead until your arms are fully extended.

5. Lateral Raises

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12

Instructions: Stand with your arms at your sides and lift them out to the sides until they are parallel to the floor.

6. Front Raises

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12

Instructions: Stand with dumbbells in front of your thighs and raise them straight in front of you to shoulder height.

7. Calf Raises

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15-20

Instructions: Stand with feet hip-width apart and raise your heels off the ground, then lower back down.
