Chest and Triceps Workout

Workout Routine

1. Bench Press

Sets: 3 | Reps: 8-10

Instructions: Lie flat on a bench, grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width, and lower it to your chest. Press back up to the starting position.

2. Incline Dumbbell Press

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12

Instructions: Set the bench to a 30-45 degree angle, hold dumbbells at shoulder height, and press them up until your arms are fully extended.

3. Chest Flyes

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12-15

Instructions: Lie on a flat bench, hold dumbbells above your chest, and lower them out to the sides in a wide arc.

4. Push-Ups

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-15

Instructions: Keep your body straight, lower yourself until your chest nearly touches the floor, then push back up.

5. Tricep Dips

Sets: 3 | Reps: 8-10

Instructions: Use parallel bars or a bench, lower your body by bending your elbows until your arms are at a 90-degree angle, then push back up.

6. Overhead Tricep Extension

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12

Instructions: Stand or sit with a dumbbell held with both hands above your head. Lower the weight behind your head, then raise it back to the starting position.

7. Skull Crushers

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12

Instructions: Lie on a bench with a barbell or dumbbells, lower the weights towards your forehead by bending your elbows, then extend back to the start.
