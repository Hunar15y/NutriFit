HIIT Workout

Workout Routine

1. Jumping Jacks

Duration: 30 seconds

Instructions: Jump with legs spread and arms overhead, then return to standing position.

2. Burpees

Duration: 30 seconds

Instructions: From standing, squat down, kick feet back, do a push-up, return to squat, and jump up.

3. High Knees

Duration: 30 seconds

Instructions: Run in place, bringing knees up towards your chest as high as possible.

4. Mountain Climbers

Duration: 30 seconds

Instructions: In plank position, alternate driving your knees towards your chest quickly.

5. Squat Jumps

Duration: 30 seconds

Instructions: Squat down, then explode upwards, jumping as high as you can before landing back in the squat.

6. Plank Jacks

Duration: 30 seconds

Instructions: In plank position, jump feet out and in, similar to jumping jacks.

7. Rest

Duration: 30 seconds
