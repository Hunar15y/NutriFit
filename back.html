Back Workout

Pull-Ups

3 sets of 6-10 reps

Focus on full range of motion and controlled descent.

Barbell Rows

3 sets of 8-12 reps

Keep your back straight and pull towards your waist.

Lat Pulldowns

3 sets of 10-15 reps

Engage your lats and avoid using momentum.

Seated Cable Rows

3 sets of 10-12 reps

Squeeze your shoulder blades together at the end of the movement.

Deadlifts

3 sets of 6-10 reps

Maintain a flat back and engage your core.
